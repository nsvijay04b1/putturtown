Train facility
( Railway station code - PUT )


The first railtransport in andhrapradesh is from puttur to
renigunta,such a historical place is puttur.

Puttur station had suffered bomb blast by naxlaties on April 1999
and reconstructed again. It has got 3 platforms, 1 footover bridge,
under bridge and flyover bridge.

You can reach puttur by train from Chennai , Kanchipuram, Pondichery, Tirupati, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune
and Mumbai.

Puttur has got computerised online reservation system and tatkal booking facility.

Puttur railway has got Railway Telecom , PW office and Railway poilce station.
Puttur is the only major station for Nagari, Satyavedu constitencies and 10's of small towns  and100's of villages
to board trains for Mumbai/Pune and Hyderabad.

In NH 205, a major station between tirupati  and chennai. A survey is being  made  for a new lane from puttur to
chennai near gummudipudi, if that project is comepleted, puttur will be junction

Bus facility

Puttur has got Bus stand in the center of the town which serves
1000's of people commuting from many villages around per day.

Puttur has it's own bus depot which serves Puttur and Nagari buses together.

You can reach directly to puttur by bus from Hyderabad, Bangalore, vellore,
Chitoor, Tirupati, Sri kalahasthri, Kadapa, Kurnool, Kanchipuram, Chennai,
Tiruthani, Arakkonam, Nagari, Satyavedu, Nagalapuram  and many more cities, towns and villages

To Puttur Kattu Hospital
(natural bone setting hospital)

"Puturu Kattu" hospital locally called as Esalapuram kattu( a village,
2 KMs from Puttur railway station).

It is connected 24/7 with auto services waiting outside railway station.

Puttur railway station also has a facility to lodge bone fracture patients at reasonable costs.

Puttur railway station is connected with Mumbai/Pune, Hyderabad, chennai and Bangalore.

Puttur is 20 Kms away from Renigunta Junction by bus with connectivity every 5-10 mins, where trains from
major parts of india stop at Renigunta.

Puttur bone hospital is 25 Kms away from Tirupati International Airport .
